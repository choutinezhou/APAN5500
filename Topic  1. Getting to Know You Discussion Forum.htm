    Detailed Instructions

    This is your space to introduce yourself to the class. This is something that lives on the site in advance of the class and takes the place of me going around the room and slowing down the class. In your introduction post, please include answers to a few of the following questions:

    • What is your name?
    • Where are you joining from geographically? (New York City or elsewhere in the US or abroad?)
    • What is your program of study?
    • What is your educational background? (Where did you go to college? What did you study? Do you have an advanced degree?)
    • Are you currently working in addition to taking classes, or were you working before starting your program of study? If so, in what industry?
    • Do you have plans or ideas for what may come next in your educational/career path? (Further graduate study or pursuing a different career path?)
    • Do you have any particular interests at the intersection of analytics and visualization, or substantive areas of interest in terms of what you hope to get out of the course?
    • Do you have any visual design or artistic skills or hobbies?
    Please post your introduction before our first Live Session. Once you have posted your introduction, please check back throughout the week to greet your classmates. If possible, please make all additional comments to this page before the end of the first week.
